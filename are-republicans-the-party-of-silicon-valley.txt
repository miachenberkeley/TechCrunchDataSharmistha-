Andrew Keen is the author of three books: Cult of the Amateur, Digital Vertigo and The Internet Is Not The Answer. He produces Futurecast, and is the host of Keen On. So how does the Republican party view the importance of Silicon Valley and innovation in the 2016 Presidential election?According to Sean Spicer, the RNCs chief strategist, innovation is central to the Republicans chances in 2016.Spicer argues that the Republicans  with their pro business, anti regulation slant  are the natural party of Silicon Valley.Scratch a startup entrepreneur, he says, and youll find a conservative  especially in the area of jobs and economic opportunity.And its this commitment to the innovation of disruptive companies like Uber and Airbnb, Spicer hopes, which will make 2016 a successful year for Republicans.Many thanks to CALinnovates and Lincoln Initiativefor helping to produce this interview.
