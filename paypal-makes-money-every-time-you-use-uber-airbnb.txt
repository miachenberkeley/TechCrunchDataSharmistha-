PayPal has been going through some big changes since separating from eBay last year. The company recently introduced a redesigned appand is now monetizing its popular peer-to-peer payments app, Venmo. Already with 18 million users,PayPal is expanding itsOne Touch checkoutto 120 new countries.And they are doubling down on Braintree, which powers the mobile transactions on Uber, Airbnb and StubHub. TechCrunch sat down with senior vice president Bill Ready to find out about whats in store for PayPal, and why hes not worried about Apple Pay.
