In this podcast, B.V. Jagadeesh, a Silicon Valley veteran who co-founded and led iconic companies such as Exodus Communications,talks with Mayfields Navin Chaddha about bootstrapping Exodus in its early days.Jagadeesh also speaks about the importance of taking the long view; building a culture focused on customer acquisition & retention; and the key role played by mentors  including his own Kanwal Rekhi. He shares the value of tapping into organizations like TIE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) for entrepreneurs at the earliest stages.
